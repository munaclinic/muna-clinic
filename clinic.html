Muna Clinic

Muna Clinic Private Limited is a registered healthcare company based in Pokhara-2, Kaski. It’s named after my childhood nickname — a reminder that healthcare should feel personal, familiar, and rooted in trust.

Today: A Platform for Ideas

While I complete my MD residency, Muna Clinic is my side project for sharing knowledge. I write blog posts, explore topics in emergency medicine and primary care, and document lessons from my training. Think of it as a growing library of thoughts, clinical experiences, and reflections — open to anyone who finds them useful.

Tomorrow: A Practice for the Community

Once I finish my MD, Muna Clinic will activate as an appointment-based consultation service, with a focus on:

  • Emergency and acute care guidance
  • General practice and family medicine
  • Primary care for all ages
  • Community health awareness and camps

The goal is simple: bring timely, compassionate, evidence-based care to Pokhara and beyond, built on the same values I carry into every patient interaction — kindness, clarity, and competence.

Legal: Registered under the Companies Act, 2063, with registration and constitutional documents in place. The clinic complies with all relevant Nepali laws and medical regulations.
